About

Nandamuri Balakrishna (born 10 June 1960), also known as Balayya or NBK, is an Indian actor, film producer and politician known for his works in Telugu cinema. Balakrishna is an elected member of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly from Hindupuram constituency since 2014. The son of actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh N. T. Rama Rao, Balakrishna made his debut as a child artist at the age of 14 with the film Tatamma Kala (1974). In 2025, the Government of India honoured him with third-highest civilian award in India, the Padma Bhushan. Balakrishna is a recipient of three state Nandi Awards, three SIIMA Awards, and an IIFA Award. In 2012, Balakrishna was the chief guest at the 43rd IFFI. Currently, he serves as the Chairman of Basavatarakam Indo-American Cancer Hospital and Research Institute in Hyderabad.

Career

Balakrishna made his debut at a very young age in 1974 with a cameo in the film "Tatamma Kala", which was directed by his father, NTR. However, he truly established himself as a leading actor in Telugu cinema with his breakthrough roles in the 1980s.

Major Milestones in Film Career:
• First Major Role: His first major role was in "Kannappa" (1976), where he played the lead role, marking the beginning of his successful career in the industry.
• Success in the 1990s: Balakrishna starred in several hit films throughout the 1990s, but his role in "Narasimha Naidu" (2001) became a defining moment of his career, solidifying his status as a mass hero.

Achievements

Notable Films:
• "Samarasimha Reddy" (1999): This action-packed drama was a massive success and is considered one of Balakrishna's most iconic films.
• "Simha" (2010): This film was a major hit, and Balakrishna received widespread acclaim for his portrayal of a righteous and strong character.
• "Legend" (2014): Directed by Boyapati Srinu, this movie became a box-office hit and is still one of his biggest commercial successes.
• "Bhakta Prahlada" (1993): Balakrishna portrayed the role of Prahlada, which was a memorable mythological role and earned him praise for his emotional depth.
• "Lion" (2015): Another action film that was well-received by his fans.

Other Contributions:
• Mythological Roles: Apart from contemporary roles, Balakrishna is known for his portrayal of mythological characters, particularly in films like "Bhakta Prahlada" and "Rama Rajyam" (2011).
• Television: Balakrishna is also known for his popular TV show "Meelo Evaru Koteeswarudu", a Telugu adaptation of "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?".

Educational Life

Balakrishna completed his schooling in Hyderabad. He pursued his higher education in the city as well, though details about his specific academic qualifications are not extensively shared. Despite limited formal education details, he was always immersed in the world of cinema and politics due to his family background.

Marriage Life

Balakrishna is married to Vijaya Nirmala (not to be confused with the legendary actress Vijaya Nirmala). They got married in 1982 and have three children together.

Children: They have three children – a son named Nandamuri Mokshagna, who is interested in acting and could potentially join the film industry, and two daughters – Teja and Madhavi.

Balakrishna is known for being a private person when it comes to his family and personal life. However, he has a strong family bond and is seen attending family functions and supporting his children.
